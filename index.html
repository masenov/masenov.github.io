Biography

I am a PhD candidate at The University of Edinburgh and Heriot-Watt University, part of the RAD group , supervised by Dr. Subramanian Ramamoorthy and Dr. Kartic Subr. I am working on the interection of Robotics, Machine Learning and Physics, i.e. how can we solve highly dynamical tasks in uncertain environments by incorportaing physical inductive bias. This allows for performing tasks such as localizing a gas leakage with a UAV, stopping an unknown bouncing ball mid-air, etc., by incorportaing useful bias for wind and gas dispersion; free fall and restitution; etc. I am also a committee member of EdIntelligence and cohort representative for the Robotics RAS CDT.
